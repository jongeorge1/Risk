Many men with prostate cancer have no symptoms and do not feel unwell. Most prostate cancer is slow-growing and, following successful treatment and/or monitoring, may cause no symptoms for many years, if at all.

If you have any of the symptoms listed below you should contact a Healthcare Professional.

  • Side effects of treatment
  • Unexpected change in your PSA level
  • Infrequent PSA tests
